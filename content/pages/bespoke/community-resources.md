### Your Journey Starts Today!

| Category | Name | Website | Description |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Legal Assistance | Pennsylvania Immigration Resource Center (PIRC) | [www.pirclaw.org](http://www.pirclaw.org) | Offers legal services, education, and advocacy to assist immigrants in securing their legal rights in the U.S. |
| Education | Welcoming Center for New Pennsylvanians | [www.welcomingcenter.org](http://www.welcomingcenter.org) | Provides job placement, workforce development, and educational programs to immigrants and refugees in PA. |
| Health Services | Nationalities Service Center (NSC) | [www.nscphila.org](http://www.nscphila.org) | Offers health and wellness services, including mental health support and medical case management for immigrants and refugees. |
| Community Support | HIAS Pennsylvania | [www.hiaspa.org](http://www.hiaspa.org) | Provides legal services, resettlement assistance, and community integration programs for immigrants and refugees. |
| Employment Services | International Rescue Committee (IRC) in Philadelphia | [www.rescue.org/united-states/philadelphia-pa](http://www.rescue.org/united-states/philadelphia-pa) | Offers job readiness training, employment placement, and economic empowerment programs for refugees and immigrants. |
| Youth and Education | The Pennsylvania Migrant Education Program | [www.center-school.org/mep](http://www.center-school.org/mep) | Provides educational programs and advocacy to support migrant youth and their families in achieving educational success. |
| Cultural Integration | Global Pittsburgh | [www.globalpittsburgh.org](http://www.globalpittsburgh.org) | Engages and enriches the community through international connections, cultures, and social programs for immigrants. |
