## Careers at Growth Journey

We're growing—join us to help our community thrive by expanding access to care; bilingual clinicians are especially welcome.

# Bilingual Part-Time Marriage and Family Therapist (MFT) Contractor – LMFT Track

**Growth Journey Therapy | Philadelphia, Pennsylvania**

Growth Journey Therapy is seeking a bilingual, part-time Marriage and Family Therapist (MFT) contractor in Pennsylvania who is on track toward LMFT licensure and interested in providing thoughtful, culturally responsive therapy in a flexible private practice setting.

This opportunity is ideal for a therapist who already has a full-time job and is looking for a meaningful part-time therapist role with scheduling flexibility, or for a clinician who is unable to work full-time but still wants to maintain a steady caseload. It is also a strong fit for bilingual and immigrant therapists who feel passionate about serving their own communities and offering therapy in a language other than English.

We are especially interested in clinicians who want to be part of a private practice without having to build and manage one on their own. This role offers the opportunity to focus on clinical care while being part of an established practice, without carrying the full responsibility of marketing, operations, and the day-to-day business side of private practice. It may also be a great fit for therapists who may want to transition into their own private practice in the future, once fully licensed, but who are not looking to take that step yet.

At Growth Journey Therapy, we value relational, systemic, trauma-informed, and culturally responsive care. We welcome clinicians who are warm, reliable, reflective, and committed to supporting individuals, couples, and families from diverse and often underserved communities.

## Position Type

- 1099 Independent Contractor
- Part-time caseload
- Flexible scheduling
- Telehealth-based and/or hybrid depending on practice needs

## Required Qualifications

- Master's degree in Marriage and Family Therapy or a closely related field
- On track toward LMFT licensure in Pennsylvania
- Bilingual required: fluent in English and at least one other language, with the ability to provide therapy in a language other than English
- Willingness to build and maintain a part-time caseload
- Experience or interest in working with individuals, couples, and families
- Comfort with telehealth and outpatient mental health work
- Strong clinical, documentation, communication, and professional skills
- Interest in relational, systemic, and trauma-informed therapy

## This Role May Be a Good Fit For Clinicians Who

- Already work full-time and want a flexible part-time therapy opportunity
- Cannot commit to full-time work at this stage
- Want to serve their own language and cultural communities
- Want private practice experience without having to manage the business side on their own
- May want to transition into solo private practice in the future, once fully licensed

## About Growth Journey Therapy

Growth Journey Therapy is a culturally responsive practice that values relational healing, community care, and trauma-informed services. We serve a diverse client population and welcome clinicians who bring humility, care, and commitment to their work.

## To Apply

Please send your resume and a brief introduction sharing your language fluency, availability, clinical interests, and where you are in your licensure journey.
