**When:** Tuesdays, 7-9pm on Zoom

**Dates:** April 7, 14, 21, 28 and May 5, 12

**Price:** Free

**Where:** Zoom

Link will be sent after registering

## Group will be conducted in Spanish with a bilingual therapist

- Parenting support and practical guidance for new and expecting caregivers
- Pregnancy and postpartum emotional support
- Caregiver stress, anxiety, sadness, and overwhelm
- Understanding young children's emotional and behavioral development
- Communication and connection between caregivers and children
- Managing tantrums, big feelings, and challenging behaviors
- Healthy routines, coping skills, and emotional regulation at home
- Screen time, tablets, cell phones and setting technology boundaries
- Helping children stay engaged in play and daily routines when less reliance on internet based distractions
- Building support and connection with other caregivers

## Group will be facilitated by GROWTH JOURNEY bilingual therapist

Open to all Spanish-speaking NSC clients who are caregivers of children 0-8 years old. All immigration statuses are welcome!
