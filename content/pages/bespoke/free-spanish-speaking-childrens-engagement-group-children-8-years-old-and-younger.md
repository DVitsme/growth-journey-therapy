## Group will be conducted in Spanish with a bilingual therapist

- Social skills development and making friends
- Play-based learning and structured peer interaction
- Emotional regulation and coping with big feelings
- Building communication, confidence, and self-expression
- Creative activities including drawing, games, and guided play
- Learning routines, listening skills, and following directions
- Psychoeducation through age-appropriate learning, including early ABCs and foundational skills
- Support for children with learning differences and developmental needs
- Welcoming children with autism and ABA-related support needs
- Safe opportunities to practice peer engagement, teamwork, and emotional expression

## Group will be facilitated by Growth Journey bilingual therapist

Open to all Spanish-speaking NSC clients who are 8 years old or younger. All immigration statuses are welcome!
