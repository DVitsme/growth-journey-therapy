### Your Journey Starts Today!

## We Accept Most Insurance for Mental Health Services

Navigating the financial aspects of therapy can often feel overwhelming, but at Growth Journey Therapy, we strive to make this process as seamless and straightforward as possible. We believe that financial concerns should never stand in the way of accessing quality mental health care. That's why we're committed to providing clear information about insurance coverage and payment options, ensuring you can focus on your therapy journey without undue stress about costs.

## Accepting Major Insurance Programs

Growth Journey Therapy is proud to accept most major insurance programs available in Pennsylvania. Our goal is to make our services accessible to as many individuals as possible by working with a wide range of insurance providers. Currently, we accept plans from:

- United Healthcare
- Optum
- Oscar Health
- Cigna
- Evernorth
- Aetna

By accepting these insurance providers, we hope to accommodate a broad spectrum of clients, facilitating their access to our specialized therapy services. We understand the importance of utilizing your insurance benefits, and our staff is here to assist you in navigating your coverage details. We encourage you to reach out to us with any questions you may have regarding your specific plan and coverage. We also offer very limited sliding scale services on a case by case basis.

### Exclusions: Medicare and Medicaid

It's important to note that at this time, Growth Journey Therapy does not accept Medicare or Medicaid. We understand this may be a concern for some potential clients, and we are continuously evaluating our insurance partnerships to expand our accessibility. For those who are on Medicare or Medicaid, we encourage you to contact us for recommendations to other resources or providers who may be able to assist you.

## Personalized Therapist Placement

Understanding that each individual's concerns are unique, we take great care in matching you with a therapist who specializes in your specific area of need. Our team comprises compassionate, skilled professionals with expertise in a wide range of therapeutic areas, including stress, anxiety, depression, immigration anxiety, and the impacts of generational trauma. This personalized therapist placement ensures that you receive the most effective and specialized care possible, fostering an environment of healing and growth.
