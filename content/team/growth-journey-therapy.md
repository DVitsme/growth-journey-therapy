<p>Growth Journey Therapy is an independent, bilingual (English and Spanish), culturally-affirming mental health practice serving individuals, families, and couples in Philadelphia and across Pennsylvania via telehealth. Founded by Nelsery De Leon, LMFT, the practice is dedicated to compassionate, culturally aware care for immigrant, BIPOC, and other historically underserved communities.</p>
