<p>Karla is a bilingual therapist who provides therapy in English and Spanish to children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families. She brings a warm, grounded, and culturally responsive presence to her work, supporting clients navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, relationship challenges, life transitions, stress, and substance use concerns.
<span class="more-text-dots">...</span>
<br/><br/>
<span class="more-text-content">
Her clinical approach is rooted in person-centered therapy, CBT, mindfulness, trauma-informed care, and strengths-based practices. Karla believes therapy should be a safe and collaborative space where clients feel heard, respected, and supported as they build coping skills, reconnect with their strengths, and move toward meaningful change.
<br/><br/>
Karla earned her Master of Science in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Saint Joseph's University and also holds a Master of Science in Organizational Development and Leadership with a concentration in Organizational Psychology. She is a Licensed Associate Professional Counselor in Pennsylvania and brings additional experience in healthcare, patient care coordination, training, leadership, and supporting individuals and families through complex systems.
